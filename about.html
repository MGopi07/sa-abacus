SA Academy

SA Abacus is part of SA Academy India. The programme was originally founded in Malaysia in 1999 and launched in India in August 2003. Now SA Abacus has grown into a large team passionate team of professionals who believe that every child is potentially smart. We believe that systematic training can make a huge impact on every child. We have offices across all major cities across India and we are constantly learning to improve ourselves. During the Covid lockdown we transformed ourselves into a completely online training organization and now can train children effectively both online and also offline in the classroom.
